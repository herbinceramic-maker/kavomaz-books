Editorial Policy

Kavomaz Books is built around reader-first recommendations. Our goal is to help readers discover practical books, journals, audiobooks, and learning tools without hiding commercial relationships.

How we choose books and tools

We prioritize relevance to the reader’s problem: overthinking, burnout, procrastination, anxiety, emotional exhaustion, productivity, journaling, reading habits, and family-friendly children’s books. We may include Kavomaz titles, other authors’ books, audiobooks, courses, journals, and reading tools when they fit the topic.

Reader-first standards

  • Recommendations must be useful, clear, and relevant to the page topic.
  • Affiliate relationships must not override editorial usefulness.
  • We avoid exaggerated claims, fake scarcity, and misleading medical promises.
  • Self-help content is educational and does not replace professional medical, psychological, legal, or financial advice.

Transparency

Some links may be affiliate links. If a reader purchases through those links, Kavomaz Books may earn a commission at no extra cost to the reader. Affiliate compensation does not change the price paid by the reader and should not control the editorial angle of an article.

Corrections

If we find outdated information, broken links, inaccurate product details, or unclear disclosures, we aim to correct them as soon as reasonably possible.
