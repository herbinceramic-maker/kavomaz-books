Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Kavomaz Books may use cookies and similar technologies for website functionality, analytics, affiliate tracking, advertising measurement, and reader experience.

Types of cookies we may use

  • Essential cookies: used for basic website functionality and security.
  • Analytics cookies: used through tools such as Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity to understand traffic, page performance, and reading behavior.
  • Affiliate cookies: third-party partners such as Amazon, Bookshop.org, Awin, CJ Affiliate, Impact, Rakuten, and similar networks may use cookies after you click an affiliate link.
  • Advertising and retargeting cookies: future campaigns may use platforms such as Pinterest, TikTok, Meta, LinkedIn, Microsoft Ads, or Google Ads.
  • Preference cookies: used to remember choices such as cookie banner preferences.

Affiliate links

When you click a partner link, you may be redirected to a third-party website. That third party may set its own cookies according to its own privacy and cookie policies. Kavomaz Books may earn a commission if you make a qualifying purchase at no extra cost to you.

Managing cookies

You can accept or decline optional cookies in the cookie banner. You can also clear or block cookies in your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect analytics, personalization, or affiliate tracking.

Updates

This policy may be updated as new affiliate programs, analytics tools, or advertising platforms are added to the website.
