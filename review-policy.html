Review Policy

Our reviews and comparison guides are designed to help readers make better choices. We explain why a book or tool may be useful, who it is for, and when it may not be the right fit.

Review criteria

  • Reader fit: how well the recommendation matches the reader’s emotional or practical need.
  • Clarity: whether the book or tool is understandable and usable.
  • Practical value: whether it gives the reader actionable ideas, routines, prompts, or insight.
  • Buying options: whether readers have ethical choices such as Amazon, Bookshop.org, audiobook platforms, or other approved partners.

Affiliate independence

We may earn affiliate commissions, but affiliate availability alone is not enough reason to recommend a product. We avoid recommending products that do not fit the reader’s need.

Review copies and partnerships

Authors, publishers, and brands may contact Kavomaz Books about review copies, collaborations, rights questions, or affiliate partnerships. Receiving a review copy does not guarantee coverage or a positive recommendation.

Product details

Prices, availability, editions, ratings, and retailer offers can change. Readers should confirm current details on the retailer’s website before purchasing.
