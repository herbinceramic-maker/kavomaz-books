Do you need these tools?

No. These tools are optional. They should only be considered if they make reading calmer, easier, or more consistent. A quiet room and a simple book are enough to start.

FAQ

Do I need these tools to benefit from the books?

No. These are optional supports. The most important step is choosing the right book and creating a quiet moment to read.
