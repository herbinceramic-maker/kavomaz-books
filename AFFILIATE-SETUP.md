# Kavomaz Books V5 Affiliate Setup

## Priority order
1. Amazon Associates / Amazon KDP links for Kavomaz books.
2. Bookshop.org affiliate account for alternative book buying paths.
3. Awin for global lifestyle, education, wellness and productivity brands.
4. CJ Affiliate for established publisher partnerships.
5. Impact for direct brand partnerships.
6. ShareASale, Rakuten and PartnerStack as secondary networks.

## Important
Do not add fake affiliate links. Add final tracking links only after each account is approved.

## Where links are prepared
- `/affiliate.html`
- `/blog/affiliate/*.html`
- Existing book pages under `/books/`

## GTM events
Use `affiliate_partner_click`, `amazon_click`, `sample_download`, `product_click`, `external_link_click`, and `affiliate_hub_view`.
