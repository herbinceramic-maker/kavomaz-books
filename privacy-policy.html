Privacy Policy

Kavomaz Books may use Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics 4, Microsoft Clarity, affiliate tracking links, email subscription tools, standard server logs, and future advertising pixels to understand traffic, improve content, and measure reader engagement.

Information we may collect

  • Basic website analytics such as pages viewed, device type, approximate location, referral source, and interaction events.
  • Affiliate click data such as outbound partner clicks and product button clicks.
  • Email address if you choose to contact us or join the newsletter.
  • Technical information such as browser, operating system, and standard log data.

How we use information

We use data to improve the website, understand which articles help readers, measure affiliate button performance, respond to messages, and manage future newsletter updates.

Third-party services

The website may use Google Tag Manager, GA4, Microsoft Clarity, affiliate networks, email marketing providers, and future advertising platforms such as Pinterest, TikTok, Meta, LinkedIn, Microsoft Ads, or Google Ads. These services may process data according to their own policies.

Affiliate tracking

When you click a partner link, you may leave Kavomaz Books and visit a third-party website. That partner may use cookies or tracking technologies to attribute a qualifying purchase or lead.

Your choices

You can manage cookies in your browser, decline optional cookies in the cookie banner, avoid submitting personal information, or contact us with privacy questions at books@kavomaz.com.
